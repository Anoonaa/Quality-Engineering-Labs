## About

Lab 1 introduces the **fundamentals of Quality Engineering**. Students build a foundation in testing principles, quality assurance methodologies, and the role of a QA engineer in modern software teams. Content for this lab is delivered through the **Red Academy LMS**.

---

## Objectives

- Understand core quality engineering concepts and terminology
- Learn foundational software testing methodologies
- Explore the QA engineer's role in the software development lifecycle
